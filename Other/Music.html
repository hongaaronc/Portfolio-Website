    Original Music Compositions

    I occassionally have tried writing songs as a hobby. I don't really have much formal music composition training, so I pretty much go off of what sounds good to me. Here are some original pieces I made.

    "Ambient Rock Demo" was written in my first year out of college. It was inspired by the Silent Hill 1 theme, mostly its instrumentation.

    "Chiptune Demo" was written in my third year of college. It is inspired mostly by Anamanaguchi and Coldplay.

    "Ambient Rock Demo" was written in my second year of college when I was working on AT SUNDOWN. I was mostly trying to write out thoughts on how a Wild-West themed soundtrack would work in the game. It is lightly inspired by the song "Red Sand (Swift)" by Bill Laurance.

    "Four Seasons of Rock" was from when I was in middle school.
