Custom Mechanical Keyboard Design

Ongoing Project

    3D Sample Renders

    I have been interested in creating my own custom keyboard design. I modelled and rendered these designs, and hope to refine them. I'd like to eventually actually make these designs ready for manufacturing and run a group buy to produce them.

    These designs were take inspiration from 80's analog synthesizers and their flip-up control panels, as well as rear-diffusers from sports cars (the fins on the back), and the clean design cues from Apple's MacBooks.
