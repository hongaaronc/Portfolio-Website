Description

The Otaku Keyboardu is inspired by a westernized view of Japan.
The aesthetic is clean and minimalist, with a mix of modern and classic styling cues.

The katakana set is geared toward touch typists - if they like the look of it, but I will also offer a set for people who don’t fall into these categories. There will be a child deal which has Latin alphanumeric characters as well as legends for the Home cluster.


Otaku Keyboardu is in the Signature Plastics’ DSA keycap family. Keycaps will be Double-Shot ABS.

Features

• Doubleshot ABS
• DSA profile
• Colors: RDD, BBI, GGK, YBP
